## 🧠 Key Features

### Intelligent Cleanup
The software uses advanced language models to polish your dictation. It corrects grammar, fixes stutters, and formats your speech into readable sentences. You receive a finished draft every time.

### Global Hotkeys
You control the dictation process from any program. Whether you use a web browser, a word processor, or an email client, the hotkey triggers the microphone. You never need to leave your active window to record.

### Private Clipboard
Your data remains secure. The application clears your clipboard automatically after you paste your text. This prevents unauthorized access to your previous transcriptions. 

### Field Focus
Dictator identifies the active text cursor. It injects your transcribed words precisely where your cursor sits. This prevents errors when working across multiple open windows.

## 🔧 Troubleshooting

If the application fails to record your voice, check the following items:

*   **Microphone Access:** Open your Windows Settings, navigate to Privacy & Security, and select Microphone. Ensure the application has permission to use your hardware.
*   **API Key Validity:** Check your settings menu. An expired or incorrect API key prevents the cloud service from processing your audio.
*   **Internet Stability:** A slow connection causes a delay in transcription. Ensure your network shows a strong signal.
*   **Volume Settings:** Check the sound settings in your control panel. Make sure your input level shows activity when you speak.

## 🛡️ Privacy Policy

Your voice data travels to the cloud provider only for the duration of the transcription process. The software does not store your audio files on your local drive. You choose your cloud provider, which allows you to review their specific data handling policies. The dictator app acts as a bridge between your hardware and the processing service.
